In a Customized Exam, you can decide the following values of the application

  1. Subjects you intend to take: between 1 and 4 inclusive
  2. Timeframe for the Examination: between 300 and 14400
  3. Number of Questions for each subject: 10, 20, 30, 40, 50

You must also enter two of your names with a space in between them

The keyboard shortcut for this button is Ctrl +C
